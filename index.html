Totoro

The bus stop is an important facet in our community. Thousands of people visit one each day. This is a convenient place to not only get information about where the bus is, but also information on the community around the bus stop.

This site experiments with ways to visualize real-time arrival of buses at a given bus stop. These could be used in the physical bus stop, or maybe at a business that is very near by.

Totoro is named after the iconic bus stop image used often to represent the movie, My Neighbor Totoro.
